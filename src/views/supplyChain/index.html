The Qitmeer Network is divided into four key eras: Mecca (awakening), Medina (innovation), Umayyad (stability), and Abbasid (prosperity).

Qitmeer's tokenomics integrates value carriers from these periods. The Umayyad era marked the mainnet launch, where 42,108,530 MEER were allocated during Genesis Mapping. The Umayyad 1.0 "Majority Game Model" allocated 12,159,120 MEER as staking rewards, but only 7,426,492 MEER entered circulation. The remaining 4,732,628 MEER serve as the ecosystem development fund.

Currently, all tokens from Genesis Mapping, staking rewards, and development funds are in circulation. New MEER is generated solely through POW mining, with ecosystem and future funds released as needed.
